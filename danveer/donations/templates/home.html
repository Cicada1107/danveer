Welcome to Daanveer

Connecting generosity with need.

Everyone one in our society deserves the right to be taken care of. Daanveer aims to bridge the gap between people who want to donate to the needy who could benefit from such people's help through NGOs.

How does it work?

Well its simple:

1. Register yourself as a donor or a beneficiary and setup your profile.
2. Donate or request a donation or just explore the community to see how you fit in.
3. Anything is a donation. From life saving medicines to the unused copies lying on your shelf.
4. Donations are mapped as nearest-location-first basis to NGOs/beneficiaries who then distribute it to the needy.
5. The beneficiaries nearest to you, will be notified of your donation and vice versa, thus eliminating 3rd party logistics.
6. Once a beneficiary claims your donation, they can contact you on your provided mail-id.

Become an actual hero. Save real lives, here.
